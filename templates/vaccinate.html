Vaccine Information

Vaccines are essential for maintaining the health of your goats. Here are some common vaccines and their benefits:

CD&T (Clostridium Perfringens Types C & D and Tetanus)

This vaccine protects against enterotoxemia (overeating disease) and tetanus. It's crucial for young kids and adult goats alike.

Rabies

The rabies vaccine is important for goats, especially if they are in areas where rabies is common. It prevents the deadly rabies virus.

Caseous Lymphadenitis (CL)

This vaccine helps prevent Caseous Lymphadenitis, a bacterial infection that causes abscesses in goats.
